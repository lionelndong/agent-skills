# Cheatsheet — $100M Offers

## The Value Equation (never rewrite)
```
        Dream Outcome  ×  Perceived Likelihood of Achievement
Value = ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────
            Time Delay  ×  Effort & Sacrifice
```
↑ top two · ↓ bottom two. Drive the bottom toward zero → value toward infinity. **The bottom two (Time, Effort) are the hard, winnable levers.**

## Order of leverage (work top-down)
**Market (Starving Crowd) > Offer Strength > Persuasion Skills.** A "great" higher lever beats a "normal/bad" lower one; a "bad" market stops everything.

## Decision rules
| When… | Do… | Because… |
|---|---|---|
| You're being compared on price | Build a GSO → category of one | Incomparable = value-driven, not price-driven |
| Tempted to discount to close | Add a **bonus** instead | Discounting trains negotiation & signals weakness |
| Choosing a market | Score Pain / Power / Targeting / Growth | Market is the highest lever |
| Under ~$10M/yr & unsure how broad | **Niche down**, then commit | Riches are in the niches; switching restarts the clock |
| Two offers satisfy the same desire | Differentiate on Likelihood, Time, Effort | Dream Outcome cancels out |
| High-cost / high-ticket fulfillment | Use **Conditional** or **Anti**-guarantee | You'd eat refund *and* fulfillment cost |
| Offer is fatiguing | Vary top-down (below) | Cheaper changes usually suffice |
| Demand not yet flowing | Over-deliver, monetize, *then* add friction | "Create flow, monetize flow, then add friction" |

## Four market indicators
1. Massive Pain · 2. Purchasing Power · 3. Easy to Target · 4. Growing. **Three markets:** Health · Wealth · Relationships.

## Build the GSO (5 offer steps)
1. Dream Outcome → 2. List every Problem → 3. Solutions list → 4. Delivery vehicles (Delivery Cube) → 5. Trim & Stack.

## Delivery Cube cheat codes
Attention (1-on-1 / small group / one-to-many) · Effort (DIY / DWY / DFY) · Live medium · Recording format · Speed · **10×-to-1/10th test**. Favor **high-value one-to-many** assets.

## The Five Enhancers (keep all distinct)
| Enhancer | Lever | Core move |
|---|---|---|
| **Scarcity** | Quantity | Limit supply → fear of loss |
| **Urgency** | Time | Deadline → decide now |
| **Bonuses** | Value | Stack value, don't discount |
| **Guarantees** | Risk | Reverse risk buyer→seller |
| **Naming** | Attention | Right people notice (MAGIC) |

## 3 types of Scarcity
Seats/slots · Bonuses · Never again. (Services: Total cap / Growth-rate cap / Cohort cap.) **Always sell out — and announce it.**

## 4 types of Urgency
Cohort-based rolling · Rolling seasonal · Pricing/bonus-based · Exploding opportunity. *Make deadlines real.* (50–60% of sales hit the last 4 hours.)

## 4 types of Guarantees
| Type | Essence |
|---|---|
| Unconditional | No-questions-asked refund (strongest; lower-ticket) |
| Conditional | Terms; aim "better than money back"; tie to success actions |
| Anti-Guarantee | All sales final + a believable reason why |
| Implied | Performance / revshare — "no perform, no pay" |
Structure: **"If not X in Y, then Z."** Name it. Hit it boldly. Stack them.

## Guarantee math
100 sales / 5 refunds = 95 net → 130 sales / 13 refunds = 117 net = **1.23× (+23%)**. Don't be emotional — do the math.

## Pricing cycles
Premium → **Virtuous cycle** (better clients, results, margin, scale). Discount → **Vicious cycle** (worst clients, no margin). Widen price-to-value by raising **value**, not cutting price. "No strategic benefit to being second-cheapest."

## MAGIC naming (use 3–5)
**M**agnet (reason why) · **A**vatar (who) · **G**oal (dream outcome) · **I**nterval (time frame) · **C**ontainer (Challenge/Blueprint/Bootcamp…). Short & punchy; test names.

## Offer-fatigue variation order (top-down = cheapest first)
Creative → Body copy → Headline/wrapper → Duration → Enhancer (free/discount) → Monetization structure (last resort). *You change the wrapper, not the offer.*

## Tells & laws
- **Fear of loss > desire for gain.** · **Fast beats free.** · **The pain is the pitch.**
- **Hormozi's Law:** "The longer you delay the ask, the bigger the ask you can make."
- **Perception is reality** — communicate every value driver or it isn't worth anything.
- Those who pay the most, pay the most attention.
