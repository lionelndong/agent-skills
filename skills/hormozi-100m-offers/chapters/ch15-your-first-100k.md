# Section V (Execution): Your First $100,000

## Core Idea
With the Grand Slam Offer built, you now have "a valuable, high margin, de-commoditized grand slam offer" — "the first building block of a wonderful business." For many, this one offer is enough to reach their first $100,000. (Charlie Munger: "The first $100,000 is a bitch, but you gotta do it.")

1. Why you must **not be a commodity**.

3. Why you should **charge a lot of money**.
4. How to charge it — the **four core value drivers** (the Value Equation).
5. How to **create your value offer in five steps**.
6. How to **stack the value**, deliver it, and make it profitable.
7. How to shift the demand curve with **scarcity**.
8. How to use **urgency** to decrease the action threshold.
9. How to strategically use **bonuses** to increase demand.
10. How to **reverse buyer risk** with a creative guarantee.
11. How to **name it** so it resonates with your avatar.
