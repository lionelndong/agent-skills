## Core Frameworks & Mental Models

### What this journal is
Hormozi's **private handwritten journal (© 2023)** for the making of **$100M Offers** and **$100M Leads**, scanned to 266 image pages. His "Dear Reader" note: *"Don't judge me for my 100 bad ideas. Judge me for my 1 good one — because it's the only one that matters. … You'll see me wrestle with ideas in front of your eyes. … You'll also see some ideas I cut from the books but still hold merit. … P.S. You cannot lose if you do not quit."* He says he curated "only the most legible" of his notes and grouped them to show **concept evolution**. Treat it as a **structural/historical artifact** — it confirms the *skeletons and named components* of both books as he drafted, re-drafted, and re-ordered them.

### $100M Offers journal — legible skeleton (Ch 2)
**Purpose drawn on the page:** "Print Money / Make Profitable Offers / Acquire Customers At A Profit." **Sections:** O Start Here → I **Client (→ Customer) Financed Acquisition (CFA)** → II Ultimate Monetization Structure → III/IV New Client Sources / Promotions (**Paid · Earned · Owned Media**). **Promotion sequence:** ATTENTION → PROMOTE → LIQUIDATE → MAXIMIZE → STABILIZE. **Frameworks drafted ("FIRST DRAFT OF THE …"):** **Grand Slam Offer** (Guarantee · Naming · Monetization · Problems) · **Value Equation** ((Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood) ÷ (Time Delay × Effort & Sacrifice) — *exact; never alter*) · **Naming** (Offer Headline Formula + **MAGIC**: Magnetic reason · Avatar · Goal · Interval · Container) · **Gift Wrapping an Offer** ("same bundle, different wrapper") · **Guarantees** ("reverse the biggest perceived risk, specifically") · **Enhancing** (Before/After · Problems/Solutions · Bundle · Bonus) · Scarcity · Urgency · Sales-to-Fulfillment Continuum · Virtuous vs. Vicious Cycle · Supply & Demand.

### $100M Leads journal — legible skeleton (Ch 3)
**Sections:** I Define the Target → II Where/When + Filters → III Give/Ask (the 4 Activities) + **Rule of 100** → IV Evaluate → V Scale/Expand Market (**Broader · Up · Down · Adjacent**) → VI Leverage/Execution (**OPM/OPT**). Feynman epigraphs.
- **The Core Four** (how to get leads): **Warm Outreach · Cold Outreach · Post Content · Run Paid Ads** (Know-you/Don't × Private/Public).
- **Lead Getters** (others advertise for you): **Customers · Affiliates · Employees · Agencies.**
- **Lead pipeline:** Stranger → Uncontacted → Contacted → Engaged → Qualified → Sold (Advertise → Incentivize → Nurture → Sell).
- **Content unit:** **Hook → Retain → Reward** (retain via **Lists / Steps / Stories**).
- **Core math (drafted):** **Lead Flow = Attention × Pressure** (through Filters); **LTGP : CAC** (e.g., 30:10 = 3:1) — **New Way** (upsell so profit > CAC → cash stays positive) vs. **Old Way** (cash dips negative).
- **Hiring:** **Internal vs. External Core Four**; **Performance Diamond** (Communication · Training · Motivation · Circumstances).
- **Referrals:** **Golden Ratio = Referred ÷ Churned**; milestones **Activate → Testimonial → Refer → Ascend.** **Affiliates:** **Whisper · Tease · Shout.**

### Levers Hormozi repeats across both books
**Better leads/offers:** More · Better · New · Cheaper · Reliable. **Expand a market:** Broader · Up · Down · Adjacent. **Leverage:** Lead Getters (1 you → lead getters → a recruiter of lead getters) + OPM/OPT. **Always:** one platform first, then expand; pull one named lever at a time.
