# Glossary — The $100M Journal

*Terms below are the framework/section names that appear **legibly** in Hormozi's handwritten journal. Definitions are kept to what the journal shows; for full definitions use the `hormozi-100m-offers` and `hormozi-100m-leads` skills. Chapter refs in parentheses.*

## The journal itself
**The $100M Journal** — Hormozi's private handwritten notebook (© 2023) documenting the *making of* $100M Offers and $100M Leads; 266 scanned pages, mostly illegible, "curated [to] only the most legible" notes. (Ch 1)

**Concept evolution** — Hormozi's term for how the journal is organized: grouped by book to show ideas being wrestled with, drafted, and re-ordered. (Ch 1)

**"100 bad ideas / 1 good one"** — His framing for the journal: only the one good idea matters; the rest is the cost of finding it. (Ch 1)

**"FIRST DRAFT OF THE …"** — The recurring page heading marking each framework as a draft, not finished teaching. (Ch 2)

## $100M Offers journal (Ch 2)
**Grand Slam Offer** — Drawn with components **Guarantee · Naming · Monetization · Problems** plus the value workings.

**Value Equation** — **(Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood of Achievement) ÷ (Time Delay × Effort & Sacrifice).** Reproduce exactly; do not alter.

**Client Financed Acquisition (CFA)** — The journal's name (Section I) for what the books/Lost Chapters call **Customer** Financed Acquisition; same concept, earlier label.

**Ultimate Monetization Structure** — Section II of the Offers skeleton.

**New Client Sources / Promotions** — Section III/IV; split into **Paid Media · Earned Media · Owned Media** (+ Referrals).

**ATTENTION → PROMOTE → LIQUIDATE → MAXIMIZE → STABILIZE** — The promotion/liquidation sequence on the Start-Here skeleton.

**Enhancing (the offer)** — **Before/After · Problems/Solutions · Bundle · Bonus(es).**

**Offer Headline Formula** — Naming building blocks (Avatar/Time/Container + Enhancer like FREE/% OFF/Guarantee/Flash Sale/Giveaway); "you do not need every piece — this is a framework."

**MAGIC Headline Formula** — **M**ake A **M**agnetic Reason · **A**nnounce the **A**vatar · **G**ive them a Goal · **I**ndicate a time Interval · **C**omplete with a **C**ontainer word.

**Gift Wrapping an Offer** — "Same bundle, different wrapper": the name is the wrapper; value comes from the stack.

**Price to Value Discrepancy / Point of Cancellation** — The gap between price and value, and where a customer cancels.

**Virtuous vs. Vicious Cycle (of Price)** — How raising/lowering price moves perceived value, results, demandingness, service level, and sales-team conviction.

**Sales to Fulfillment Continuum** — Terms/Duration/When-They-Pay/Bonuses/Discounts/Guarantees/Actions mapped against Easy↔Hard to Sell and Easy↔Hard to Fulfill.

**How To Create An Attractive Guarantee** — Take the biggest perceived risk in the sale and reverse it (their time, emotion, outside costs); the more specific, the more it stands out.

**Supply & Demand Curve** — Price-vs-units scenarios to find the highest-potential price.

## $100M Leads journal (Ch 3)
**Core Four** — The four ways to get leads: **Warm Outreach · Cold Outreach · Post Content · Run Paid Ads**, on a Know-You/Don't × Private/Public 2×2.

**Lead Getters / Four Lead Types** — Others who advertise for you: **Customers · Affiliates · Employees · Agencies.**

**Lead Stages** — **Stranger → Uncontacted → Contacted → Engaged → Qualified → Sold**, via **Advertise → Incentivize → Nurture → Sell** (with a NO branch).

**Advertising Cycle** — **Offer · Avatar · Medium → Advertise → Channel · Platform · Audience → Engaged Leads.**

**Hook → Retain → Reward** — The atomic content unit (H-R-R), nested from short-form up to Book → Chapters → Paragraphs.

**Three Retention Structures** — **Lists · Steps · Stories.**

**Lead Flow = Attention × Pressure** — Lead generation reduced to attention × pressure, run through **Filters.**

**Lead Distillation Process** — Raw → Filtered → Refined leads (Fishing / Gold Panning / Diamond Mining).

**Media types** — **Paid Media · Earned Media · Owned Media.**

**Media Matrix** — Read/Watch/Listen × Short/Medium/Long (+ Live/Recorded, In-Person/Virtual).

**More · Better · New · Cheaper · Reliable** — The levers to get better leads on any channel.

**Broader · Up · Down · Adjacent** — The directions to expand a market.

**Rule of 100** — 100 primary actions/day for 100 days across Warm Reach Outs / Cold Reach Outs / Content / Paid Ads (minutes or dollars); bonus: across more than one Core Four activity.

**LTGP : CAC** — Compare Lifetime Gross Profit to Customer Acquisition Cost (e.g., 30:10 = 3:1) to judge whether a channel funds its own growth; drawn as **New Way vs. Old Way** cash-flow.

**Internal vs. External Core Four** — Same Core Four applied to getting **employees** (Internal) vs. getting **customers** (External).

**Performance Diamond** — Diagnose dropped performance via **Communication · Training · Motivation · Circumstances.**

**Golden Ratio (Referrals)** — **Clients Referred ÷ Clients Churned**; with the **Referral Growth Equation** (monthly referred − monthly churned = compounding growth).

**Four Simple Customer Milestones** — **Activate → [Testimonial] → Refer → Ascend** (order may vary).

**Whisper · Tease · Shout** — Affiliate-launch cadence.

**OPM / OPT** — Other People's Money / Other People's Time (the Leverage/Execution section).
