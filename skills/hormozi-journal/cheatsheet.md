**Hiring:** **Internal vs. External Core Four**; diagnose with the **Performance Diamond** (Communication · Training · Motivation · Circumstances).

**Referrals:** **Golden Ratio = Clients Referred ÷ Clients Churned**; **Referral Growth Equation** (monthly referred − churned = compounding); **Milestones: Activate → Testimonial → Refer → Ascend.**

**Affiliates:** "you get affiliates [with a] hook, they get customers"; launch cadence **Whisper · Tease · Shout.**
