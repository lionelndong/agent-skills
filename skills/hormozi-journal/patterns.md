# Patterns — The $100M Journal

*The journal is a behind-the-scenes artifact, so its "patterns" are mostly **how to read a creator's working notes** plus the legible **skeletons** of two books. Each is stated as "use X when Y." For the actual frameworks-as-patterns, see the `hormozi-100m-offers` and `hormozi-100m-leads` skills.*

## Reading-the-journal patterns

### Legible-only extraction
- **Use when** mining a scanned handwritten notebook for value.
- **Pattern:** trust **printed labels, headings, and named frameworks**; treat illegible strokes as unknown. Capture nothing you can't actually read. Fix obvious OCR typos (e.g., "Clicnts"→"Clients") but never invent words.
- **Why:** most of these 266 pages OCR to noise; the signal is in the labels and skeletons.

### "100 bad ideas / 1 good one"
- **Use when** judging your own (or a creator's) raw brainstorming.
- **Pattern:** don't grade the process by its bad ideas; grade it by whether it produced the one good idea. Publishing the mess is fine because the winner is what matters.

### Skeleton-first drafting
- **Use when** writing a book, course, or system.
- **Pattern:** draw the **section skeleton** (Section O/I/II/…), then re-draw and re-order it many times before filling in content. The journal shows both books built this way — the order changed repeatedly before it stabilized.

### Name-as-wrapper
- **Use when** packaging an offer.
- **Pattern:** *"same bundle, different wrapper"* — keep the value stack fixed and re-name it into the most magnetic headline using the **Offer Headline / MAGIC** building blocks. The name changes attractiveness, not value.

## $100M Offers skeleton (legible)
- **Use when** you want the genesis/structure of $100M Offers.
- **Pattern:** Purpose = *"Print Money / Make Profitable Offers / Acquire Customers At A Profit."* Sections: **O Start Here → I Client(Customer) Financed Acquisition → II Ultimate Monetization Structure → III/IV New Client Sources / Promotions (Paid · Earned · Owned Media).** Promotion sequence: **ATTENTION → PROMOTE → LIQUIDATE → MAXIMIZE → STABILIZE.**
- **Frameworks present:** Grand Slam Offer (Guarantee · Naming · Monetization · Problems), Value Equation, Naming (Offer Headline / MAGIC), Guarantees ("reverse the biggest risk, specifically"), Scarcity & Urgency, Sales-to-Fulfillment Continuum, Virtuous vs. Vicious Cycle, Supply & Demand.

## $100M Leads skeleton (legible)
- **Use when** you want the genesis/structure of $100M Leads.
- **Pattern:** Sections: **I Define the Target → II Where/When + Filters → III Give/Ask (the 4 Activities) + Rule of 100 → IV Evaluate → V Scale/Expand Market → VI Leverage/Execution.** Epigraphs from **Feynman** ("if the evidence disproves it, it is wrong").
- **Frameworks present:** Core Four (Warm/Cold Outreach, Post Content, Paid Ads), Lead Getters (Customers/Affiliates/Employees/Agencies), Lead Stages (Stranger→…→Sold), Advertising Cycle, Hook→Retain→Reward (+ Lists/Steps/Stories), Lead Flow = Attention × Pressure, LTGP:CAC (New Way vs. Old Way), Rule of 100, Internal vs. External Core Four, Performance Diamond, Referrals (Golden Ratio + Growth Equation), Affiliates (Whisper/Tease/Shout).

## Levers the journal repeats (both books)
- **Get better leads / offers:** **More · Better · New · Cheaper · Reliable.**
- **Expand a market:** **Broader · Up · Down · Adjacent.**
- **Grow leverage:** through **Lead Getters** (1 you → lead getters → a recruiter of lead getters) and **OPM / OPT.**
- **Use when** deciding what to change next on a channel or offer: pull one named lever at a time, on one platform first, then expand.
