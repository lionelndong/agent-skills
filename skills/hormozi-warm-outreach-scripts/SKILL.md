## Core Scripts & Frameworks

### Warm Setter Call (B2B — responded lead) — book in 36 hours
Permission ("is now a bad time?") → their **20–30s overview** + compliment → trigger → **big goal + timeline (the motivation lever)** → recap + surface pain → "share my .02?" → diagnose + tease the fix → **assign a pre-call video** (their choice to watch → more watch) → **book within 36 hours** → **cc all founders/partners** → confirm + "is there any reason I won't see you at X?"

### Inbound Assessment Call (B2C — front-end offer)
Open as **qualification** ("we can't take everyone") → discovery **PAIN → DIG 1 → DIG 2 → future pace** → "you're just like our best-result clients" → book the assessment with scarcity → **take a card on file to hold the spot** (kills no-shows) → text address + a **selfie**.
- **Three card-on-file overcomes**: normalize (like reserving hair/nails) · reason-why (only ~10% showed without it) · joking tone.
- **Phone best practices**: smile first; say **"John!?"** not "is this John?"; no multitasking; if they answer, finish it; **Double Dial** (voicemail → call right back).

### Organic DM Strategy (convert lukewarm followers)
DM in **3 separate messages**: Hey (name) · how you found them · personalized compliment · question. Respond with **A-C-A** (Acknowledge → Compliment → Ask; use voice memos). Deploy the **short fill-in offer**: *"I help (ideal customer) get (dream outcome) in (time) without (effort & sacrifice) and (↑ perceived likelihood). Does anyone you like come to mind?"* → no? "Anyone you hate?" Then move to a call — **speed is king**.

**The law**: *Volume wins every time — 100 reach outs per day.* The first interaction's only job is to get an interaction.
