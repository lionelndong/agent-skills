# Cheatsheet — Warm Outreach Scripts

## Phone best practices (every call)
- **Smile first.** Print the script. **No multitasking.**
- Say **"John!?"** — never "Is this John?"
- **If they answer, finish it** (take the CC on the phone).
- **Double Dial** — voicemail → hang up → call right back.

## Warm setter call (B2B) — the flow
Permission → their 20–30s overview + compliment → trigger → **big goal + timeline** → recap + pain → "share my .02?" → diagnose + tease fix → **assign the video** → **book in 36h** → **cc all founders** → confirm + "any reason I won't see you at X?"
- **Lever**: the timeline ("by when, and why").
- **Buy-in**: the pre-call video (they choose to watch → more watch).

## Inbound assessment call (B2C) — the flow
Open-as-qualification → discovery **PAIN → DIG 1 → DIG 2 → future pace** → "you're just like our best clients" → book assessment with scarcity → **card on file to hold the spot** → text address + selfie.

### Card-on-file overcomes
1. **Normalize**: "like reserving hair/nails — you put a card to hold the time."
2. **Reason-why**: "without it, only ~10% showed."
3. **Joking**: "afraid I'll go shopping? or that you won't show?"

## Organic DM → call
- **DM (3 separate messages)**: Hey (name) · how you found them · personalized compliment · question.
- **Respond with A-C-A**: Acknowledge → Compliment → Ask (steer to offer). Use **voice memos**.
- **Short fill-in offer**: "I help (ideal customer) get (dream outcome) in (time) without (effort & sacrifice) and (↑ perceived likelihood). Anyone you like come to mind?" → no? "Anyone you hate?"
- **Speed is king.** Follow up with story slide-ups · memes · ^^.

## The law
**Volume wins every time — 100 reach outs per day.** The first interaction's only job is to get an interaction.
