# Chapter 3: Organic DM Strategy — Turn Lukewarm Followers Into $$$

## Core Idea
A free play to convert **people who follow you / engage with your content but don't act on your CTAs**. Overall process: lead follows you → a setter with a trusted profile reaches out → builds value → gets them on a call / calendar → turns into money. *"Simple scales. Fancy fails."*

## The DM Framework
Look for people who follow/like posts on your CEO/company/personal accounts. Open with a 4-line DM:
1. **Hey (firstname)**
2. **How you found them** ("Found you off Alex's account / saw you just followed")
3. **Personalized compliment** ("Saw you owned a brick-and-mortar store, looks amazing")
4. **Question** ("How long have you been in the game?")
*Pro tip: send it in three separate messages* (not one block). **The purpose of the first interaction is to get an interaction.**

## The A-C-A Framework (how to respond)
- **Acknowledge** what they said — restate in your own words (shows active listening): "Two kids… and you're an accountant…"
- **Compliment** — tie it to a positive character trait: "Wow! Supermom! So hardworking — managing a full-time career and two kids…"
- **Ask** another question — lead toward your offer:
  - Therapy/coaching: "Do you get time for yourself?"
  - Fitness/weight loss: "Do you have time for workouts?"
  - Cleaning: "Do you have anyone who keeps your house tidy?"
*Pro tip: use **voice memos** to break up the conversation.*

## The Short Fill-in Offer (for DM/text/email)
> "I help **(ideal customer)** get **(dream outcome)** in **(time period)** without **(effort & sacrifice)** and **(increased perceived likelihood of achievement)**. Does anyone you like come to mind?"
- If they say no → "Anyone you hate? (ha)"
- Works for email, texts, DMs, calls, in-person — just fill in the blanks.

## Get on a Call — Speed Is King
Once you get a response → set up a call. "Do you mind if I give you a quick call? I might have some free resources for you as well." **Speed is king.**

## Follow Up Like a Pro (keep it simple + natural)
1. Story slide-ups · 2. Memes · 3. Up arrows (^^).

## Key Concepts
- **The first interaction's only job is to get an interaction** — don't pitch in message one.
- **A-C-A** keeps the conversation human while steering toward the offer.
- **Volume wins every time — 100 reach outs per day.**

## Key Takeaways
1. DM = Hey + how-you-found-them + compliment + question (3 separate messages).
2. Respond with A-C-A; use voice memos; deploy the short fill-in offer.
3. Move to a call fast — speed is king — and do 100 reach outs/day.
