# Chapter 2: The Inbound Assessment Call Script (B2C) + Phone Best Practices

## Core Idea
A phone script for a **B2C lead who took a front-end offer** — run discovery on the pain, then book an in-person/virtual **assessment** and **take a card on file** to hold the spot (which kills no-shows).

## Phone Best Practices (do these every call)
- **Start with a SMILE.** Print the script and keep it in front of you.
- **Assume you have the right person**: say **"John!?"** — never "Is this John?" or "Can I speak to John?"
- **No multitasking** — full attention; treat every call as a first impression on a potential lifelong client.
- **If they answer, finish the conversation** — callbacks rarely happen; try to take the CC on the phone.
- **Double Dial** — if it goes to voicemail, hang up and call right back so they know you're not a telemarketer. People are far more likely to pick up the second call.

## The Script Flow
1. **Open** (taking the call for the specialist): "Hey NAME? … I'm taking this call for [Name], our specialist. You've probably heard of [COMPANY]? … one of the FASTEST growing [type] in the region with HUNDREDS of [results]."
2. **Frame the call as qualification**: "Since we've had such an overwhelming response for our [FRONT END OFFER], we're unable to take everyone — so this call is to ensure you're a **GOOD FIT** and get you scheduled for your assessment, sound good?"
3. **Discovery — PAIN, DIG, GET REAL**:
   - "What MOTIVATED you to sign up? Are you trying to [goal 1], [goal 2], [goal 3], or all 3?"
   - Current situation → GOAL → "Which bothers you the most? How long has this been bothering you? What bad habits got you here? What negative impact has [current state] had on your life?"
   - **DIG 1**: "How has it affected your kids? Marriage? Work? Body image? Sleep?"
   - **DIG 2**: "I need you to be REAL with me… so you're telling me you've had NO negative impacts?"
   - **Future pace**: "Ten years down the road, if you're still dealing with this, who are you going to be? Is that who you want to be?"
4. **Tie back + book**: "Based on what you told me, you're JUST like the people who get the BEST results because [reason]… we've helped over 1,000 people just like you… let's get you set up with your assessment. Morning or evening?"
5. **Scarcity + card on file**: "Let me see what's available — we've been so slammed… Oh great, one spot open [day/time]. All I need is **whatever card you want on file to hold your appointment** — because we offer this at such a deep discount, we require a card to hold your spot. Debit or credit?"
6. **Confirm + text**: "I'll text you our address, parking, and a **selfie of me and [Name]** so you know who to look for."

## The Three Card-on-File OVERCOMES
- **#1 (normalize)**: "Just like getting your hair, nails, or lashes done — you put a card down to reserve the time, since our coaches meet you for free instead of paying clients. It's just how we've always done it. Fair enough?"
- **#2 (reason why)**: "We used to do it without a card, but only ~10% showed — no skin in the game. Now we can actually get people results. What's the first 4 digits of the card?"
- **#3 (joking tone)**: "Is the fear that I'm going to go on an online shopping spree? Or… is it that you don't think you'd show up?"

## Key Takeaways
1. Open as qualification → PAIN/DIG/future-pace discovery → book the assessment with scarcity.
2. **Take a card on file** to hold the spot — it eliminates no-shows.
3. Have the three overcomes ready; confirm with a text + a selfie.
