# Glossary — Warm Outreach Scripts

**A-C-A Framework** — Reply method for DMs/conversations: **Acknowledge** (restate what they said), **Compliment** (tie to a positive trait), **Ask** (the next question toward your offer). (Ch 3)

**Assessment** — The booked appointment a B2C inbound script schedules (held with a card on file). (Ch 2)

**Card on File** — Taking a card to hold the appointment slot; eliminates no-shows ("we used to have ~10% show without it"). (Ch 2)

**Double Dial** — If a call goes to voicemail, hang up and immediately call again so they know you're not a telemarketer; the second call gets picked up more. (Ch 2)

**DIG (DIG 1 / DIG 2)** — Discovery prompts that deepen the pain (impact on kids/marriage/work; "be REAL with me"). (Ch 2)

**Fill-in Offer (short)** — "I help (ideal customer) get (dream outcome) in (time) without (effort & sacrifice) and (increased perceived likelihood)…" — works across DM/text/email/call. (Ch 3)

**Future Pace** — "Ten years down the road, if you're still dealing with this, who are you going to be?" (Ch 2)

**Overcomes** — Prepared responses to the card-on-file objection (normalize / reason-why / joking tone). (Ch 2)

**Setter** — The person making the first contact and booking the call (a.k.a. coordinator). (Ch 1, Ch 3)

**"Smile first"** — Always start a call with a smile and excitement. (Ch 2)

**The timeline lever** — The prospect's "by when" date; the motivation lever to drive action. (Ch 1)

**The video (homework)** — A pre-call training video the prospect commits to watching; raises show + close. (Ch 1)
