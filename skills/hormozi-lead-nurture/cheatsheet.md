# Cheatsheet — $100M Lead Nurture

## The one job
Maximize **30-day show rate** — the window *after advertising, before sales*. "If they do not show, they cannot buy." Drivers = **the Four Pillars** (+ Execute).

## The three metrics
| Metric | Definition |
|---|---|
| **Schedule Rate** | % of engaged leads who schedule |
| **Show Rate** | % of scheduled leads who show |
| **Throughput** | % of engaged leads who show (Schedule × Show) |
*Ex: 100 leads × 50% schedule × 50% show = 25% throughput. A 20–40% lift ≈ 2x–3x profit at 20% margins.*

## The Four Pillars (do in this order — Availability & Speed are biggest)
| # | Pillar | One-liner | Default targets |
|---|---|---|---|
| I | **Availability** | "If leads cannot schedule, they do not show." | 7 days/wk · ~9am–9pm · **4 slots/hour** · inbound+outbound+self |
| II | **Speed** | "Money loves speed." | Contact **<5 min** · book **≤72 hrs** · respond instantly |
| III | **Personalization** | "Personalize, don't pressure." | Qualify → score → route → segment → incentivize → prove |
| IV | **Volume** | "Volume negates luck." | **7+** touches, front-loaded · reminders · BAMFAM |
| — | **Execute** | "None of this works unless you do." | Culture + rank show/close/call-to-close |

## Friction dial (Availability)
- Too **few** appointments → **remove** friction (cut steps, pre-fill fields, obvious times).
- Too many **bad** appointments → **add** friction (video, sales letter, price on page, delayed scheduler).

## Speed thresholds
- First contact: **<5 min** (extra credit <60 sec). *Faster contact = fewer follow-ups.*
- First appointment: **3 days out max** (5 if forced); **pull forward to same day** ("right now" shows at 100%).
- Stats that justify it: **391%** lift if <60 sec · **7x** qualify if <1 hr · **78%** buy from whoever responds first.

## Pull-forward call → 5 outcomes
1. No response → remove (or double-book if in-person).
2. Unqualified → remove; give free value / downsell.
3. **Qualified + free now** → guaranteed show; close or **hot handoff**.
4. Qualified → **pull appointment forward**.
5. Qualified → **re-confirm** time.
*Hot handoff = three-way message + edify the closer.*

## Lead scoring (Personalization)
Red = unqualified · Yellow = less qualified (**"yellows are the new gold"**) · Green = prime.
Route **best leads → best closers**. Score criteria from your **best customers'** actions + demographics.

## Incentives: Push vs Pull
| Type | Timing | Mechanism | Example |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Push (gift)** | Before arrival | Reciprocity; no strings (keep it either way) | "$5 gift card — check your email" |
| **Pull (A/B)** | After arrival | They choose; both options assume they show | "Face cream or hand lotion, waiting tomorrow?" |
*A/B works only if: they want it · it connects to your offer · you really incurred the cost. You can A/B anything (quantity, start date, flavor, time slot, color…).*

## The Volume cadence (front-loaded)
| Day | Actions |
|---|---|
| **Day 1** | Call <5 min → **double-dial** → voicemail → text → +2 double-dial+text (hours apart) |
| **Days 2–3** | Call 2x/day (early + late); text after the 2nd |
| **Days 4–7** | Call + text 1x/day |
| **Week 2+** | → long-term nurture (free value + soft CTA; re-engage → restart) |
*Get an **auto-dialer** (2–3x efficiency). Obey local contact laws. Double-dial: 2nd call beats first-call blocking.*

## Reminders
- **Automated** (label them!): immediate confirm + **24h / 12h / 3h** out. Add the **area code** you'll call from (2x pick-up) + lead's **local time zone**.
- **Manual** (real phone): **night before / morning of / ~60 min prior**. Several **short** messages, not one block. Pattern: **Acknowledge – Compliment – Ask the next question** (use it to qualify + pull forward).

## BAMFAM
"Book-A-Meeting-From-A-Meeting." Never "we'll circle back." Open the calendar, book the next step **in real time**. Dodging the next call = an objection — handle it.

## Tells & smells
- Not hearing "wow, that was fast!" daily → you're **too slow** to contact.
- Show rate drops (esp. one rep) → check **time stamps / response speed** first.
- Calendar full of no-shows → booked **too far out**; tighten to ≤72 hrs.
- Reps cherry-pick green leads → losing the **yellows** that convert in volume.
- Team obsessed with close rate only → track **show, close, AND call-to-close**.

## Pro tips
- **Blue (real-human) texts** out-respond green; get team iPhones.
- **Local area-code wrapping** → far higher pick-up rates.
- Best **setters** are usually best **closers** — same skill set; onboard low-risk.

## Scope
Medium-term nurture only (interest → show). Long-term follow-up (emails/podcasts/content) and **Proof** are separate playbooks.
