# Chapter 1: Campaign Overview, Affiliate Prize Structure & Event Logistics

## Core Idea
This handbook is the **affiliate marketing kit** for the *$100M Money Models Free Virtual Book Launch*. Affiliates send their **unique referral link** to drive live registrations; the more people who register and show up through that link, the bigger the **tiered prizes** both the affiliate and their referrals unlock.

## Frameworks Introduced
- **The Affiliate Referral Loop**: register via the affiliate's magic link → both the affiliate *and* the registrant win shared prizes. The recurring close in every asset: *"We both win when you use my link."*
  - When to use: every piece of copy in this kit drives to one action — register through *my* link.
  - How: lead with what the *viewer* gets (the free secret project + the training), then add the affiliate bonus as a shared upside ("if we hit 10, we both get…").
- **The Affiliate Prize Ladder** (the incentive engine — preserve the exact tiers):
  - **10 referrals** → *70 Profit Tactics Audiobook* + entry into the Vegas HQ day drawing.
  - **Top 50 affiliates** → guaranteed day at Alex's Vegas HQ to ask the audience's questions + the recording.
  - **Top 10 affiliates** → 30-min fireside chat with Alex, live-streamed to the affiliate's audience, with everyone getting the recording.
  - When to use: this ladder is the affiliate's *self-interest* hook, restated in every email and outreach script.
  - How: frame milestones as collective ("when *we* hit 10…") so referrals feel they're helping unlock bonuses for themselves too.

## Key Concepts
- **The Magic Link**: registrants get a free magic link emailed on sign-up; it grants access to the live virtual event. The affiliate's referral link is the tracked version of this.
- **Live-only gating**: the "secret project" goes *only* to people watching live — the core scarcity lever across the whole campaign.
- **Shared-prize framing**: bonuses unlock for *everyone who used the link*, not just the affiliate — turns referrals into co-promoters.
- **The Secret Project**: a 4-year project Alex gives away free to live attendees, teased as *"better than an NFT, less than a Bitcoin."*

## Mental Models
- Think of the affiliate as a **co-host, not a salesperson** — they're inviting friends to an event they're personally attending ("I'm going. If you wanna come with…").
- Use **social proof as the size anchor**: "Over 1 million people registered" / "the biggest entrepreneur event of the year."

## Anti-patterns
- **Hard selling**: this is a *free* event — pressure undercuts the "come with me" frame. Lead with the invite, not a pitch.
- **Burying the link**: every asset ends with one clear CTA to register through the affiliate's link; never assume they'll find it.
- **Promising prizes as already-won**: tiers are conditional ("*when* we hit 10") — keep them aspirational, not guaranteed up front.

## Key Takeaways
1. One action drives the whole kit: **register through the affiliate's referral link** and show up live.
2. The **prize ladder (10 / Top 50 / Top 10)** is the affiliate's recruiting fuel — restate it everywhere.
3. **Live-only access** to the secret project is the campaign's master scarcity lever.
4. Frame everything as **"we both win"** so referrals self-promote.
