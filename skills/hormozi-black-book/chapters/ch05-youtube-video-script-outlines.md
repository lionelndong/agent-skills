# Chapter 5: YouTube Video Script Outlines (2 Versions)

## Core Idea
Two **timestamped video script outlines** an affiliate can film to promote the event — one *teaching/problem* angle and one *personal/authority* angle — each engineered to hook in the first 30 seconds and end on the affiliate's registration link.

## Frameworks Introduced
- **Shared Visual Elements (both videos)**: screen-share of Alex's social posts (greenscreen style) · the affiliate's own business results/screenshots · a calendar-reminder setup on camera · the register link displayed throughout *and* in the description.
- **Video 1 — "Why Most Businesses Fail at Making Money (And How to Fix It)"** (8–12 min):
  - **Hook (0:00)**: *"95% of businesses are leaving money on the table every single day. Here's why."*
  - **Beat map**: Opening Problem (0–:30, owners chase customers but ignore spend) → Personal Story (:30–2:00) → The Money Model Concept (2:00–4:00; explain simply, 1–2 examples like *restaurant upselling dessert / gym membership tiers*, note most use only 1 of the 4 types) → The Big Reveal (4:00–6:00; Alex perfected 4 Money Models, his companies use all 4 together) → Secret Project Tease (6:00–8:00; NFT/Bitcoin line, live-only, 4 years) → CTA (8:00–10:00; event details + why register through *your* link) → Closing (10:00–12:00; reinforce "this only happens once").
- **Video 2 — "I'm Going to Alex Hormozi's Biggest Event Ever (You Should Come)"** (6–10 min):
  - **Hook (0:00)**: *"Over 1 million people registered for this event. Here's why I'm going and why you should too."*
  - **Beat map**: Opening Authority (0–:30, your credibility) → Event Announcement (:30–1:00) → Why I'm Excited (1:00–3:00; what you've learned from Alex, specific results) → What We'll Learn Together (3:00–5:00; the 4 Money Models, 5 mystery speakers — some billionaires) → The Secret Bonus (5:00–7:00; 4 years, live-only, "you literally can't get it any other way") → Affiliate Benefits (7:00–9:00; explain you're an affiliate, 70 Profit Tactics audiobook at 10 referrals, Vegas HQ chance) → CTA (9:00–10:00; link in description / lower-third, set reminder, comment if going).

## Key Concepts
- **Timestamped beat structure**: each outline is a minute-by-minute shot list, not loose talking points — the affiliate films to the clock.
- **The two angles**: Video 1 = *educational/problem-first* (teach the concept, then reveal the event); Video 2 = *personal/authority-first* (your credibility and excitement carry the invite).
- **Greenscreen proof**: showing Alex's own posts on screen borrows his authority visually.
- **Persistent on-screen link**: the register link stays visible throughout *and* lives in the description and lower-third.

## Mental Models
- Open with a **stat or scale claim** ("95% of businesses…" / "Over 1 million people…") to earn the next 30 seconds.
- **Teach a little, reveal the rest live**: give 1–2 simple Money Model examples on camera, then position the full 4 as the reason to attend.
- Treat **Affiliate Benefits as a dedicated beat** (Video 2, 7:00–9:00) — explain the referral upside explicitly rather than burying it.

## Anti-patterns
- **No early hook**: both scripts spend their first 30 seconds on a hook for a reason — a slow open loses the view.
- **Over-teaching the Money Models**: explain the *concept* and a couple examples; revealing all four removes the reason to register.
- **Forgetting the persistent link**: the link must be on-screen throughout and in the description — not just spoken once.

## Key Takeaways
1. Two scripts: **problem/teaching** (Video 1) and **personal/authority** (Video 2).
2. Both are **timestamped beat maps** with hooks in the first 30 seconds.
3. Reuse **shared visuals**: greenscreen of Alex's posts, your own results, calendar setup, persistent link.
4. Tease the secret project and the 4 Money Models; make **Affiliate Benefits an explicit beat**.
