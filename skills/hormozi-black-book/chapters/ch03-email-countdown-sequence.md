# Chapter 3: Email Countdown Sequence (6 Emails)

## Core Idea
A **6-email countdown** that runs from 21 days out to live, building urgency on a fixed schedule while repeating the same two hooks (free secret project + the Money Models) and threading the affiliate's shared-prize progress through every send.

## Frameworks Introduced
- **The Countdown Cadence** (this is also the campaign's Promotion Timeline): each email is keyed to days-remaining, escalating urgency as the date nears.
  - **Email 1 — 21 Days Out** · *"The biggest entrepreneur event of the year"* → full pitch: secret project, Money Models, 1M+ registered, and the prize ladder introduced.
  - **Email 2 — 14 Days Out** · *"2 weeks until the secret is revealed"* → "we're climbing the leaderboard"; restate milestones (10 / Top 50 / Top 10) + what you get for showing up (incl. *5 mystery headliners*).
  - **Email 3 — 7 Days Out** · *"One week left + prize update"* → "so close to our first milestone"; what happens when you show up + prize update.
  - **Email 4 — 4 Days Out** · *"4 days until the big reveal"* → "set your alarm"; quick reminder + "just need a few more registrations through my link."
  - **Email 5 — 24 Hours Out** · *"Tomorrow at 9am PT / 12pm ET"* → "final call"; full recap + last push on the link.
  - **Email 6 — Live/Reminder** · *"LIVE NOW — 9am PT / 12pm ET"* → short: "we're live RIGHT NOW," join link, secret project goes only to live viewers.
  - How: each email = Subject + Preview line + Body (recap of what they get) + the affiliate prize progress + one CTA: *Register through my link here*.

## Key Concepts
- **Subject + Preview pairing**: every email ships with a matched subject line and preview text (e.g. Subject *"2 weeks until the secret is revealed"* / Preview *"Alex's 4-year project + we're climbing the leaderboard"*).
- **Progress narration**: the affiliate reports milestone progress ("we're climbing," "so close," "just need a few more") to manufacture momentum and recruit more referrals mid-sequence.
- **Recap blocks**: the "here's what happens when you show up" bullet list (Money Models · secret project · 5 mystery headliners · tactics that built his companies) is reused and lightly varied each send.
- **Email 6 is short on purpose**: at go-live, strip everything but "we're live, here's the link, live-only reward."

## Worked Example: Email 1 skeleton (21 days out)
> **Subject**: The biggest entrepreneur event of the year
> **Preview**: August 16th at 9am PT / 12pm ET - don't miss this
> **Body**: Hey [Name], something big is happening Aug 16th… Alex is launching $100M Money Models — not just any book launch. 4-year secret project, free to everyone live (better than an NFT, less than a Bitcoin). Plus the Money Models that built his companies. 1M+ already registered.
> *"Here's the cool part: when you register through my link, we're both working toward amazing prizes:"* → 70 Profit Tactics Audiobook (10 registrations) · Vegas HQ day (Top 50) · fireside chat (Top 10).
> Event Details block. **CTA**: [Register through my link here]. Sign-off: *"We both win when you use my link." [Your name]*

## Mental Models
- Run the sequence as a **tightening spiral**: same offer, shrinking time, rising urgency — info stays constant, pressure climbs.
- Use **[Name] / [Your name] / [Register through my link here]** as merge-field placeholders the affiliate fills in — the kit ships fill-in-the-blank.

## Anti-patterns
- **New information late**: don't introduce fresh value in Email 5/6 — by then it's pure reminder + link.
- **Dropping the prize progress**: the leaderboard narration is what keeps the affiliate's audience helping; carry it through every email.
- **A bloated go-live email**: Email 6 must be near-instant to read — one link, one reason.

## Key Takeaways
1. Six emails on a fixed countdown: **21 / 14 / 7 / 4 / 1 days, then live**.
2. Every email pairs a **subject + preview** and ends on the affiliate link.
3. Repeat two hooks (secret project + Money Models) and **narrate prize-milestone progress** throughout.
4. The **live email is short**; the 21-day email carries the full pitch.
