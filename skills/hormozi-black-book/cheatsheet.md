## The recurring hooks (recombine these)
Curiosity (*"better than an NFT, less than a Bitcoin"*) · Social proof (*"1M+ registered"*) · Urgency/scarcity (*"only happens once," live-only*) · Problem/solution · Direct benefit (*"spend more money, faster, over and over"*) · FREE.

## Hormozi's 8 writing rules (edit every line)
1. **3rd-grade level** — "utilize leverage" → "borrow money"
2. **Present voice** — "creating" → "create"
3. **Active not passive** — "was carried" → "we carried"
4. **No adverbs** — "shut really hard" → "slammed" (kill *very/super/way/actually*)
5. **Short sentences** — two commas → cut the middle or split
6. **Positive language** — "Don't Stop" → "Keep Going"
7. **Cut redundant words** — "simultaneously… at the same time" → "at the same time"
8. **Cut unnecessary words** — "was able to get out of his car" → "exited his car"

## Tells
- Adverb in your copy? You probably need a stronger **verb**.
- More than one comma? **Split or cut.**
- Leading with *your* affiliate payoff? **Flip it** — viewer's benefit first.
- Describing the secret project? **Stop** — keep the mystery.
