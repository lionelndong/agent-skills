## Core Frameworks & Mental Models

### The one job
Get people to **register through the affiliate's referral link and show up live**. Every asset leads with what the *viewer* gets (free event + free secret project) and folds the link in as a shared win: *"We both win when you use my link."*

### The Affiliate Referral Loop + Prize Ladder
Register via the affiliate's magic link → both parties unlock **shared, tiered prizes**:
- **10 referrals** → 70 Profit Tactics Audiobook + Vegas HQ day drawing
- **Top 50 affiliates** → guaranteed day at Alex's Vegas HQ (+ recording)
- **Top 10 affiliates** → 30-min fireside chat with Alex, live-streamed (+ recording)

Restate the ladder in every email/outreach script and **narrate progress** ("we're climbing the leaderboard") so referrals recruit more referrals.

### The Event Details block (reused verbatim)
**Sat Aug 16, 2025 · 9am PT / 12pm ET · Live on YouTube · FREE.** Live attendees get: the **secret 4-year project** ("better than an NFT, less than a Bitcoin"), the **4 Money Models**, **5 mystery headliners** (some billionaires), and the tactics that built his companies.

### One Event, Six Ad Angles (split-test set)
Same offer, six entry points — launch several, scale the winner:
1. **Secret Project** (curiosity) · 2. **Money Models Explained** ("most businesses lose money on ads") · 3. **Social Proof** ("over 1M registered") · 4. **Problem/Solution** ("your business needs a Money Model") · 5. **Urgency** ("this only happens once") · 6. **Direct Benefit** ("how to get customers to spend more money").
*Never reveal the secret project or the 4 types in the ad — that's the reason to attend.*

### The Countdown Email Sequence
6 emails on a tightening schedule: **21 / 14 / 7 / 4 / 1 days, then live.** Hold the offer constant, raise urgency, narrate prize progress, pair every send with a **subject + preview**, end on the link. The **go-live email is short**: one link, one reason (live-only reward).

### The Two-Version Swipe File (short-form)
SMS, friend texts, group posts, Slack messages, and organic social — each shipped in **two tones** (Direct vs. softer/benefit/social-proof). Pick by relationship; **link placement is channel-specific** (inline for texts/Slack; comments/bio/DM for social). Frame as a personal invite from someone already attending.

### Timestamped YouTube Scripts (2 angles)
**Video 1** (problem/teaching, 8–12m): hook *"95% of businesses leave money on the table…"*. **Video 2** (personal/authority, 6–10m): hook *"Over 1M registered…"* with an explicit affiliate-benefits beat. Both: **hook in the first 0:30**, greenscreen Alex's posts, persistent on-screen + description link, teach-a-little/reveal-the-rest-live.

### The recurring hooks
Curiosity · Social proof/scale · Urgency + scarcity (live-only) · Problem/solution · Direct benefit · **FREE**. Recombine these across every channel.

### Alex Hormozi's 8 Writing Guidelines (edit every line)
1. **3rd-grade reading level** ("utilize leverage" → "borrow money") · 2. **Present voice** ("creating" → "create") · 3. **Active not passive** ("was carried" → "we carried") · 4. **No adverbs** — they signal weak verbs ("shut really hard" → "slammed"; cut *very/super/way/actually*) · 5. **Short sentences** — two commas → cut the middle or split · 6. **Positive language** ("Don't Stop" → "Keep Going") · 7. **Remove redundant words** · 8. **Remove unnecessary words** ("was able to get out of his car" → "exited his car").
