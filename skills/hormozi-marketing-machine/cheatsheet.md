# Cheatsheet — Marketing Machine

Decision rules, the 6-point script, the 9 components, cadence, and thresholds. Keep beside you while building the machine.

## The one diagnostic
> "If I got hit by a bus today… what would keep the business growing tomorrow?"
List everything you do → systematize it → **the bottom of the list feeds the top of the list.**

## The 6-Point Testimonial Script (memorize)
1. **Internal struggle** — rock bottom; what you hated; what you couldn't do.
2. **External struggle** — your metrics then.
3. **Skepticism** — concerns about buying/joining.
4. **Overcome** — what made you do it anyway.
5. **External victory** — your metrics now.
6. **Internal victory** — best moment since; what you can do now; what you no longer must do.
- **Hook first (3–5 sec) = most important** — test a few. Have them **say their name first.**

## Award → FOUR forms of proof (per person)
| Proof | Action |
|---|---|
| **PROOF 1** | group post with real numbers + tag the owner |
| **PROOF 2** | email the video to `proof@yourco.com` |
| **PROOF 3** | unboxing video |
| **PROOF 4** | on-stage presentation (mail engraved, present generic) |
Tie the award to the community; make it **big & cool** ("don't do a boring thing").

## Cadence
- **Weekly (start-of-week checklist):** names of winners · social saves (you + tags + customers) · chat screenshots · community posts/comments + wins-scroll recording · new 5-star reviews + scroll recordings · bonus unlocks · award deliveries · reaction videos.
- **~Every 12 weeks (wave):** run competitions **between** events → 1 comp + 2 events/yr.

## Thresholds & numbers (committed in the book)
| Thing | Number |
|---|---|
| Competition winner | **most conversions over 30 days** |
| Competition frequency | **1–2× / year max** |
| Events / year | **1–2×** (do them even if you sell nothing) |
| Stage ads timing | end of day 1 / before lunch day 2 |
| Man-in-the-street | **10 core questions, 10+ answers each** |

| Optional rep reward | **$100 gift card/week** for a winning screenshot |
| Bonus unlock video | **under 5 min**, hits the 6 points |
| Hook | first **3–5 sec** |
| Lifecycle clips | **before** (sales) + **during** (1–2 support) + **after** (upsell/milestone) |

## Decision rules / tells
- **Studs vs. duds:** "Good organic performance correlates highly with good ad and outbound performance" → promote outperformers to ad creative.
- **Reward-or-it-dies:** "If you don't reward them for doing it, they won't do it."
- **Run it or it's worthless:** "Adding stuff to folders isn't gonna get you more customers — use it in your advertisements."
- **Star = customer, not you:** "Put them in the spotlight, not you" (80% of his top-50 ads had no Hormozi).
- **Too few customers?** Warm Outreach ($100M Leads) → work for free → get testimonials → spin the wheel.
