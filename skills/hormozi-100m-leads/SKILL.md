## Core Frameworks & Mental Models

### The goal: engaged leads
A **lead** is "a person you can contact." What you want is an **engaged lead** — "people who *show* interest in the stuff you sell." Engaged leads are **the true output of advertising**. Grow by getting more, better, cheaper leads, reliably. *"When you double your leads, you double your business."*

### Offers & Lead Magnets — how to get leads to engage
Advertise your **core offer** (straight for the sale) or a **lead magnet** — "a complete solution to a narrow problem" (free/low-cost) that reveals a next problem your core offer solves. **7 steps**: problem+who → how to solve (Reveal Problem / Sample-Trial / One Step of Multi-Step) → deliver (Software/Information/Services/Physical) → test name → easy to consume → darn good ("give away the secrets, sell the implementation") → CTA. A good **CTA** = what to do + a reason now (scarcity, urgency, or a made-up "reason why").

### The Core Four — the only four ways to advertise
Audience (**Warm** = know you / **Cold** = strangers) × communication (**1-to-1** private / **1-to-many** public):
- 1-to-1 Warm = **Warm Outreach** · 1-to-many Warm = **Post Free Content**
- 1-to-1 Cold = **Cold Outreach** · 1-to-many Cold = **Paid Ads**
"If you aren't getting as many leads as you want, you're not doing the core four with enough skill or volume." You only need one — max it; they compound.

- **Warm Outreach**: 10 steps — list → 100 reach outs/day → **A-C-A** (Acknowledge/Compliment/Ask) → Value-Equation offer → **first five free** → start charging → **9-word email**. (~1 customer / 100 reach outs.)
- **Post Free Content**: the audience (not the content) is the compounding asset. **Content Unit = Hook, Retain, Reward** (Hook: topics/headlines/format; Retain: lists/steps/stories; Reward: value per second). **Give:Ask → "give until they ask"** (give public, ask private); Integrated vs. Intermittent.
- **Cold Outreach**: solve 3 stranger-problems — **build a list** (scrape/buy/elbow grease) → **personalize + Big Fast Value** → **volume** (automate, follow up). Count in 100s × 100 days; ≥3x LTGP:CAC. Reliable but slow; private; hard to copy; makes a sellable business.
- **Paid Ads**: an efficiency game. Pick platform → target (lookalike + factors) → **Call Out + Value + CTA**. **Call Out** (first 5 sec; verbal: Labels/Yes-Questions/If-Then/Ridiculous Results; nonverbal: Contrast/Likeness/Scene). **Value = What-Who-When** (8 What elements = 4 value drivers + 4 opposites; Who = status via others; When = past/present/future). **Money**: Track → Lose → Print; **LTGP:CAC** is the scoreboard; **Client Financed Acquisition** (recoup get+fulfill cost in 30 days via upsells) = limitless scale.

### Core Four on Steroids — More, Better, New
- **More** = the **Rule of 100** (100 primary actions/day for 100 days).
- **Better** = test the **constraint** (biggest drop-off), one test/platform/week, log it.
- **New** = new placements > new platforms > new Core Four (beware the **Size of the Pie Fallacy**).
(Order flips to Better-More-New above $1M/yr profit.)

### The Four Lead Getters — get other people to get you leads
"Lead getters" do the Core Four *for* you (leverage). They can also get more lead getters.
- **Customer Referrals** — cheapest, exponential; build **goodwill** (Value − Price) via the **6 ways to give value**, then the **7 ways to ask**. Referrals must beat churn.
- **Employees** — the **Internal Core Four** (get talent the way you get customers); "you get rich from what you make, wealthy from what you own."
- **Agencies** — hire to **learn** a method/platform (6-month purpose + deadline), run your team alongside, then beat them and drop to consulting.
- **Affiliates** — highest leverage; **6 steps**: find ("who's got my leads?") → offer (a way to make money) → qualify (customer/expert) → pay (3-tier, max CAC) → launch (**Whisper-Tease-Shout**) → integrate.

### Get Started
**Open To Goal** (work until the *outcome* is hit, not just the action). The **One-Page Advertising Checklist**: pick a lead type → Rule of 100 / Open To Goal → do it daily → hire it out → repeat. **Roadmap (7 Levels)**: friends know → consistent → employees → referrals → more places/ways → hire killers → (billion). Promotions (bonus chapters) wrap the Grand Slam Offer: **Premium / Free / Discount**.
