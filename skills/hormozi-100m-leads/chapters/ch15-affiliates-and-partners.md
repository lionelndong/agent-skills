# Chapter 15: Lead Getter #4 — Affiliates and Partners

## Core Idea
"Nothing makes friends like money." An **affiliate** is an independent business that tells its audience to buy your stuff in exchange for money/free stuff. Unlike a referral, an affiliate has their own business and does their own advertising. Affiliates are the highest-leverage lead getter — "the lead-getter that's made me the most money." You get them by advertising to them and making them an offer — but the offer is **a fast, simple, easy way to make commissions promoting your product**.

## Why You Want an Affiliate Army
Selling 10 *customers*/mo at $10k caps you at $100k/mo. Selling 10 *affiliates*/mo who each bring you a $10k customer monthly **adds $100k of revenue every month** — same work, exponential. (ALAN scaled to **$1.7M/mo within six months** via three tiers of affiliates.)

## Framework: Build an Affiliate Army in Six Steps
1. **Find Your Ideal Affiliates** — "Who's got my leads?" The ideal affiliate has a warm audience full of people like your customers. (What do your customers buy / where do they go / what do they like → who provides that?) Make a hit-list by category (softwares, products, services).
2. **Make Them an Offer** — call out + value + CTA, but the value is: *make more money from your current customers, high chance of working (they already want it), without building/delivering/supporting it, starting tomorrow.* "Make affiliates the customer you're advertising to."
3. **Qualify Them** — get them invested so they win fast. Two ways: **Make Them a Customer** (they buy/use the product — "if they pay, they'll pay attention"; bulk purchases work great) and **Make Them an Expert** (they pay for onboarding/certification — charge **10–20% of what an average active affiliate makes in year one**).
4. **Figure Out What to Pay Them** — pay your **max allowable CAC**, via a **three-tier payout structure**: Tier 1 (25% CAC, agrees to terms), Tier 2 (50% CAC, activates), Tier 3 (100% CAC, sustains performance). Blended payout < max CAC → improves your LTGP:CAC. *Pay with product when possible* ("Sell 3, Get It Free").
5. **Get Them Advertising — Launch** with the **Whisper–Tease–Shout** method: **Whisper** (= Call Outs; curiosity, keep it mysterious, start years out — "the longer something appears to take, the more an audience values it"), **Tease** (= Elements of Value; reveal the product + date, use What-Who-When), **Shout** (= CTA; bonuses, scarcity, urgency, "first ones"). *Movie releases are the model.*
6. **Keep Them Advertising — Integrate** (easiest → hardest): they give away your lead magnet with every purchase; they promote to their audience; they directly sell your core offer.

## Worked Example: The Prestige Labs Launch
Week 1: $150,000 ("it didn't work" — $3M of inventory sat in a warehouse). Then week 4 exploded: $100k Mon, $110k Tue, $92k Wed — **over $450,000 that week**, ~300 orders/day across 400+ active affiliates. "I didn't advertise or sell any of the products at all. The affiliates did everything." ALAN + Prestige Labs did **$75M+ from 5,000+ affiliates**.

## Key Takeaways
1. Affiliates are independent businesses you offer a way to make commissions — highest leverage.
2. Six steps: find → offer → qualify (customer/expert) → pay (tiered, max CAC) → launch (Whisper-Tease-Shout) → integrate.
3. Get them invested ("if they pay, they pay attention") and activate them with launches.
