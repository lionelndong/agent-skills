# Chapters 1–2: How I Got Here & The Problem This Book Solves

## Core Idea
"Getting leads has been my get out of jail free card with no expiration date." This book solves one problem: you're not getting as many leads as you want because you're not advertising enough. The fix is to turn your business into a lead-getting machine.

## Frameworks Introduced
- **The Two Ways to Grow**: (1) Get more customers, (2) Make them worth more. **$100M Leads** is entirely about #1 — getting more customers.
- **Getting more customers = getting**: (1) More Leads, (2) Better Leads, (3) Cheaper Leads, (4) Reliably (from lots of places).
- **The core promise**: "All else being equal… when you double your leads, you double your business."

## Key Concepts
- **Advertising** = "the process of making known" — letting strangers know about the stuff you sell.
- **"I cannot lose if I do not quit"** — Hormozi's mantra through the hardest days.
- **The business model**: provide better *free* products than the marketplace's *paid* products → earn the trust of entrepreneurs over $1M/yr profit → invest in them. Free content is "trust built brick by brick."

## Worked Example: Giving Away the Secrets
Facing $150,000 in refunds in 30 days (two gym owners told their members to refund), Hormozi pivoted: he sold his gym-launch advertising system to a desperate Boise gym owner for **$6,000** ("my 'selling my secrets' sale") with zero cost to fulfill. He then sold the same licensing to the next gyms at $8,000, $10,000 — **$60,000 in a single day**. "Doing the thing that scared me most — giving away my secrets — led to the biggest breakthrough in my life." That became Gym Launch (4,500+ locations).

## Mental Models
- *The book is laid out from zero (Section II) to max (Section IV)* — first leads to a $100M+ leads machine; skills compound as you progress.
- *Masters never don't do the basics* — respecting the tried-and-true methods that got you here gets you to the next level.

## Key Takeaways
1. The bottleneck is almost always **not advertising enough** — solve it for good.
2. Grow by getting more, better, cheaper leads, reliably.
3. Double your leads and you (roughly) double your business.
