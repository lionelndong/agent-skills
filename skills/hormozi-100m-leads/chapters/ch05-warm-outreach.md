# Chapter 5: Core Four #1 — Warm Outreach

## Core Idea
Warm outreach = one-to-one contact with people who know you (your warm audience). "It's the cheapest and easiest way to find people interested in the stuff you sell — and most businesses don't do it." Reliable: "as certain as the sun rises." The fastest way to your **First Five Clients**.

## Framework: Warm Reach Outs in 10 Steps
1. **Get your list** — everyone has one (phone contacts, every email account, every social profile). "For many of you, this will be your first 1000 leads."
2. **Pick a platform** — the one with the most contacts.
3. **Personalize your message** — use something you know about them; provide value, ask for nothing.
4. **Reach out** — *to one hundred people, every day* (the Rule of 100). Up to 3 times (once/day for 3 days, or until they respond).
5. **Warm them up** — the **A-C-A framework**: **Acknowledge** (restate what they said), **Compliment** (tie to a positive trait), **Ask** (lead toward your offer).
6. **Make them an offer** — after ~3–4 exchanges, using the **Value Equation** (Dream Outcome ↑, Perceived Likelihood ↑, Time Delay ↓, Effort & Sacrifice ↓). Ask "do you know anybody who…" so it's low-pressure.
7. **Make them the easiest offer in the world** — *free* ("first five free," in exchange for: use it, give feedback, leave a review).
8. **Start at the top** — exhaust one platform, then the next, etc.
9. **Start charging** — once people start referring, you're "good enough"; swap "free" → "50% off" → and raise prices every 5 sign-ups.
10. **Keep your list warm** — give regular value; probe with Dean Jackson's **9-word email**: *"Are you still looking to [4-word desire]?"*

## Key Concepts
- **First Five Free** — "learning, not earning." You probably suck (for now); free customers forgive, give feedback, refer, and convert.
- **11 Ways to Increase Perceived Likelihood of Achievement** — own story, testimonials of people like them, volume of 5-stars, certifications, stats, experts, novelty, celebrity, guarantees, describing their pain, live demonstration.
- **The Hinge Method** — for referrals, get a three-way introduction (photo + text intro).
- **Prepay + Guarantee twist** — "would you rather pay less today or get all your money back?" (pay less = payment plan; get all back = prepay + guarantee). Most choose prepay.

## Worked Example: The Benchmarks & Money Math
~1 in 5 contacts engage → of those, ~1 in 5 take the free offer → of those, ~1 converts to paid. So **~1 customer per 100 reach outs**. At 500 reach outs/week × $400 product = **$2,000/wk → $104,000/yr** from warm outreach alone.

## Anti-patterns
- Not doing warm outreach at all ("most businesses don't" — don't be like them).
- "Undervaluing yourself" by refusing to start free — the objective now is to **learn, not earn**.

## Key Takeaways
1. Everyone has a warm list; reach out to 100/day.
2. Use A-C-A to warm them, then make a Value-Equation offer (start free).
3. Once referrals come, start charging and raising prices; keep the list warm with the 9-word email.
