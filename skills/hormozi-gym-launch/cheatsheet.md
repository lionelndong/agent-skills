# Cheatsheet — Gym Launch Secrets

## The whole book in one line
Fix the **three problems**: Broken Acquisition → **Client-Financed Acquisition** · Broken Profit Model → **Gym Profit Levers** · Hole-in-the-Bucket (attrition) → **Process/People/Product**. Then stack the **Wedding Cake** (Large Group → Semis → Supplements + Internal Plays).

## The 3 ways to grow → 3 macro levers
| Way to grow | Lever | Micro levers |
|---|---|---|
| **Acquire** | (front-end / acquisition) | offer, ads, show-up, close |
| **Ascend** | Price · Capacity | members · billing · price / sessions · duration · membership levels · attendance · space · exercise selection |
| **Resell** | Overhead + Retention | # sessions · work hours · cost of hours |

## Pricing decision rules (the Buying Curve)
| Service level | Target price/mo | Rule |
|---|---|---|
| Low service ($0–$99) | — | **Never compete here** (globo gyms win) |
| Large group (16+) | **$167–$225** (aim $199; or $39–$49/wk) | avoid the $99–$167 zone |
| Medium (5–15) = **No Man's Land** | $250–$399 | **AVOID** — split into two tiers instead |
| Semi-private (1-on-4) | **$500–$700** | priced like personal training, 4x revenue/slot |
- **Price by buyer type, not service type.** Never offer **>3 membership levels** ("a confused mind doesn't make wise decisions").
- **If you only do one thing after this book: RAISE YOUR PRICES.** (Everyone else is broke — don't copy them.)
- 20% of customers will pay 3x → adds 60% revenue serving one-fifth of them.
- **Bill weekly-advertised, 28-day cycle** → +8% revenue, zero added cost (4.33 weeks/month).

## Profit / overhead thresholds
- **80% service margin** = (Revenue − Trainer Cost) ÷ Revenue. Below it → cut sessions. Even 7% under can be **half your net income**.
- Industry margin ~**12%/year**; industry churn ~**10%/month**; industry attendance ~**85%**.
- Trainer pay: **$15–$20/hr** (× ~1.22 for payroll tax → ~$18.30).
- "Become BETTER before BIGGER." Only open a session you can fill.

## Capacity quick wins (do all at once — "cut once, cut deep")
| Lever | Move | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| Membership | unlimited → **3x/week**, call them "sessions" | ~2x capacity |
| Duration | 60-min+breaks → **45-min back-to-back** (30 if ≤15 cap) | +33% (+66% w/ 3x/week) |
| Space | **partner everyone up** | ~2x space |
| Space | convert office → floor (sell from a cubby) | +~56 EFT cap (~$11K/mo) |
| Attendance | offer slots **reverse order of popularity** + **$15 no-show fee** | smooths + new revenue stream |

## Churn → LTV (why retention wins)
- Cut churn **10% → 3%** = **3.3x LTV**. Retention cost ~**$10/customer/month** → **11:1+ ROI**.
- 9%→3% is **+300% LTV**, not +6%. Saving 25% of cancels = **+33% LTV**.
- **Leading indicators of cancellation**: responds to outreach >24 hrs late; **attends 2 or fewer times/week**. Decay: 3→2→2→1→CANCEL (intercept at Week 2).

## The retention system (Process/People/Product)
- **PROCESS**: milestones **Connect → Refer → Testify → Ascend**; the **Five Horsemen** = Reach-Outs (every 14 days, never automate) · Attendance Tracking (weekly, by Wed) · Handwritten Cards (every other an ask, P.S. only) · Member Events (every 21 days) · Exit Interviews (CLOSER re-sell, saves ~half).
- **PEOPLE**: staff by EFT-count org chart; **hire before you need it**; **sales manager LAST**; communication cycles = **Daily Huddles · Weekly Team Meetings · Weekly One-on-Ones**.
- **PRODUCT**: motivate trainers extrinsically (supplements, internal challenges, **25% semi rev-share**) then intrinsically (Six Human Needs); run the **Hamburger Workout**.

## Selling: C-L-O-S-E-R + closes
- **C**larify (the gap) → **L**abel → **O**verview (past pains) → **S**ell the vacation not the flight → **E**xplain concerns → **R**einforce (Trifecta).
- Answer "How much?" with **"It depends on your goal"** — never a number.
- Only **3 obstacles**: Stalls · Decision-Maker · Price (+ fitness questions, not real obstacles).
- **ALWAYS AGREE** ("I totally understand"), never "but." Stack closes (Jab-Jab-Uppercut-Hook).
- "Decisions don't take time, they take information." The real mistake is **not deciding**.
- Sit **shoulder-to-shoulder**, greet **by name in 10 sec**, stack signed contracts + testimonial wall.

## EFT conversion (Large Group) — 3 attempts
1. **Bribe** (Day 1): anchor program high ($599–$799), give it free with membership.
2. **Abandon Hope Sale** (Week 3 halfway): credit front-end toward a year at a lifetime rate → closes **~95%**.
3. **Last Chance** (weigh-out): give result-based credit, **change the terms, never the price** (price discrimination is illegal). **Sell to goal** — back into your number.

## High-ticket (HTP / semis)
- Start semis **only after large group is 85% full** ("dig your well before you're thirsty").
- $2K–$3K front end → **setter→closer**; **"the set is the close"** — close the **$50–$100 nonrefundable consult fee** on the phone or don't schedule (90% won't close in person otherwise).
- "The longer the runway, the bigger the takeoff." Don't volunteer the price — make them ask.

## Second-location gate (ALL four required)
$20K/month profit for **6 straight months** · staff running all ops (incl. sales) · gym grew in those 6 months · **$200K minimum in the bank**. (One super-profitable gym > many break-even ones; "scaled problems, not processes.")
