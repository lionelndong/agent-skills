# Chapter 11: Your New Gym On Paper

## Core Idea
Pull **all three macro levers at once** on a single hypothetical gym and watch the profit swing. "You may only be an hour's focused attention and a week of implementation away" from a six-figure annual swing. No math here is above multiplication and division.

## Frameworks Introduced
- **The Full Turnaround Sequence**: Step 1 — Define what you can control (everything except current EFT count). Step 2 — Price increase + billing switch. Step 3 — Increase capacity + cut overhead (all 5 capacity micro levers + session cut). Step 4 — Do the math; see the result.
- **"Cut once, cut deep"**: pull every lever in unison. "I have rarely had any client lose more than 10% when making a massive switch."
- **Sell to goal / back into your number**: decide the EFT you want, then work the price/terms/duration backward to land there.

## Worked Example: The Average Struggling Gym ($11,800 → swing of $77K–$140K/yr)
Starting stats: 100 EFTs, $118/month, 38 sessions/week (163/month), 60-min sessions, cap 16, trainer $25/hr, unlimited memberships, monthly billing, revenue $11,800, expenses $10,000.

**Step 2 (Price + Billing):** $118/month → **$39/week** (= $168/month, a $50/customer increase). Even at the max 10% loss → 90 × $168 = **$15,120** (+$3,320). *Advanced:* sell 16.6% (15 people) into 1-on-4 semis at $129/week → +$388/month each beyond what they'd pay in large group = **+$5,820/month** ($69,840/year).

**Step 3 (Capacity + Overhead):** unlimited → **3x/week**; 38 → 23 sessions/week (99/month); cap 16 → 20; partners; 60-min → **45-min back-to-back**; trainer $25 → **$18.30**. Cut from 7 to 4 sessions/day; take Friday nights and Sundays off.

**Step 4 — Three Scenarios:**
- **Scenario #1 (Advanced Perfect Swing — semis added, no attrition, calibrated pay):** +$5,800 semi revenue + $4,250 large-group increase + $1,660 payroll savings = **$11,710/month extra (~$140,520/year).**
- **Scenario #2 (Perfect Swing — no semis, no attrition, calibrated pay):** +$5,000 revenue + $3,741 cost savings = **$8,741/month (~$105,000/year).**
- **Scenario #3 (Imperfect Swing — no semis, 10% attrition, pay unchanged):** +$3,320 revenue + $3,150 savings = **$6,470/month (~$77,640/year).**

## Mental Models
- "Anyone can get rich on Excel" — but Hormozi shows where the numbers come from so you trust them, then run your own.
- "In order to win Super Bowls, you need to play fundamentals football." The levers work like a football team — blockers, passers, runners — in unison.
- Three scenarios exist to "account for whatever route you may choose… depending on your level of confidence."

## Anti-patterns
- Making changes one at a time over months (drags out the painful adjustment period).
- Not doing the math on your own gym ("be lazy and just read… for now" is allowed, but you'll leave money on the table).

## Key Takeaways
1. Pull all levers at once; an hour of math + a week of implementation can swing $77K–$140K/year.
2. Even the worst-case "imperfect" scenario nets ~$77,640/year more.
3. Decide your target EFT and back into it with price, terms, and duration.
