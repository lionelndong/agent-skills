# Cheatsheet — Cold Outreach Scripts

## The objective
Every cold script exists to **get a quick call** ("the entire point is to get on the phone"). **TKAD — Time Kills All Deals.** Finish the process in **≤48 hours**.

## Cold DM / Messenger setter
**Golden Response Rules (every message):** 1) Acknowledge/encourage — make them feel good · 2) ask the next question.
**Sequence:** Open → Relate → Transition → Clarify Goal → Label With Problem → Overview Pain → **Create Curiosity** → **Call them**.
- Cold relate = "seem like a real person, not creepy." Warm relate = reference their engagement.
- Get the call: **call now** ("here's my cell, what's yours?") > schedule (same day; **leave an open loop**).

## Written messages
- **First touch (money message):** "Are you **still** looking to **[4-word desire]**?" (drop "still" if truly cold). No images/links/frills.
- **Cold email "Are you open?":** bold claim → proof (**5–12 case studies**) → irresistible offer ("**pay $1 to make $15k**") → 15-min CTA → opt-out P.S.
- **Curiosity emails:** "I'm watching you :)" (a short video that could "double your email revenue"); "THEIR NAME <> YOUR NAME."
- **Replies:** Positive → "quick 3½-min chat, here's my calendar." Negative → "sorry to bother you, enjoy your day!"

## Follow-up sequence (multi-channel, by day)
| Day | Touch |
|---|---|
| 1 | Call → Email ("[Their Co \|\| Your Co]") → Text |
| 2 | Call |
| 3 | Email (same thread) |
| 4 | Text → Call (4–7) |
| 8 | Email "Are you busy..?" (Problem/Solution) |
*Reply to every positive AND negative.*

## Cold call BDR "set" script
Open → industry data + 2 common pains → branch (positive/negative) → offer **free 30-min consult** ("value whether we work together or not", tomorrow or day-after) → if no, fall back to email + book a touch-base → **always ask for a referral**.

## Reference voicemail
"Hi (name), it's (your name). I'm calling **in reference to (their competitor / a customer)**. Call me back at (number)."
Works because it's **short · relevant · opens a loop**.
**Don'ts:** don't say "this is (firstname)" · don't name your company · don't fail to follow up.
