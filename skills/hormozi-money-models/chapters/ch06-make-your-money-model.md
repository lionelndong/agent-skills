# Section VI: Make Your Money Model

## Core Idea
"A Money Model is a deliberate sequence of offers… to make as much money as you can as fast as you can." Ideally, **make enough from one customer to get and service at least two more in under 30 days.** It rarely looks clean — Hormozi breaks a $100M Money Model into **three stages**.

## Framework: The Three Stages
- **Stage I — Get Cash**: **Attraction Offers** get more customers for less.
- **Stage II — Get More Cash**: **Upsell & Downsell Offers** make more money from them, faster.
- **Stage III — Get The Most Cash**: **Continuity Offers** maximize their total spend.

Money Models **evolve with the business** (none start "finished" — a fully-forged model collapses a zero-stage startup):
1. Get customers reliably →
2. Make sure they pay for themselves reliably →
3. Make sure they pay for *other* customers reliably →
4. Maximize each customer's long-term value →
5. Spend as many ad dollars as you can to print money.
Each stage pays for the next. **Warning: "When your Money Model starts working, your business starts breaking"** — get someone who can build and lead the team.

## Framework: Make Your Own Money Model (4 steps)
1. **Start with an Attraction Offer** — turn strangers into customers and cover costs (Win Your Money Back, Giveaways, Decoy, Buy X Get Y Free, Pay Less Now/More Later). Finding what works can take up to a year.
2. **Pick an Upsell Offer** — push 30-day profit well above acquisition+delivery cost (Classic, Menu, Anchor, Rollover). Offer at the time of greatest need.
3. **Pick a Downsell Offer** — convert the "nos" (Payment Plan, Trial, Feature). Alternate between them in one sale.
4. **Pick a Continuity Offer** — one last sale in the 30-day window + recurring cash (Bonus, Discount, Waived Fee). Sometimes the right timing is after day 30 — that's OK.

## Worked Example: The Gym Launch $100M Money Model
- **Stage I — Decoy Offer**: free DIY decoy vs. **$16,000** done-with-you licensing → **$476K/mo in 3 months**.
- **Stage II — Classic Upsell** ("Gym Lords," $42K/yr, $6K prepay discount) + **Continuity Bonus** (community) + **Payment Plan Downsell** (seesaw: $10K down → $800/wk → start free) → **~$1.5M/mo**.
- **Then Menu Upsell + Feature Downsell** (done-for-you ads, sales training, minimum package) → **$2.3M/mo within 14 months**.
- Plus Prestige Labs' own Money Model → **$4.4M/mo by month 20**.

*(Other models given: Micro Gyms — Win Your Money Back → Payment Plan → Menu Upsell → Feature Downsell → Rollover + Lifetime Discount. Newsletter — Free Trial → Pay Less Now/More Later + Lifetime Discount, "a six-headed money-making monster." Dog Food — Buy X Get Y Free → Classic Upsell → Feature Downsell → Auto-renewal.)*

## The Important Notes
- **Perfect one offer at a time** — don't build the whole model at once; make one offer reliable, then automatic, then move to the next stage. "Measure in quarters, not weeks."
- **Raise price in stages** — start cheap to get yeses + feedback, then raise price until you make *less* money.
- **Simple Scales, Fancy Fails** — *"it's less about having 100 products to offer, and more about having 100 ways to offer your product."*
- **Affiliate products can fill Money Model gaps** — sell other people's stuff for commission (no delivery headache) when you lack an offer.
- **Turn Attraction Offers into Continuity Offers with automatic renewal** (e.g., "Buy 6 Get 6 Free" → month-to-month after 12 months).
- **Mix and match** — there are no rules; start the way he suggests, then experiment.

## Where Money Models Fit
- **$100M Offers** → *What should I sell?* (an offer so good people feel stupid saying no)
- **$100M Leads** → *How do I find these people?* (you advertise)
- **$100M Money Models** → *How do I get them to buy it?* (a Money Model)

## Key Takeaways
1. Three stages: Get Cash (Attraction) → Get More Cash (Upsell/Downsell) → Get The Most Cash (Continuity).
2. Build it one offer at a time; let each stage pay for the next; raise price in stages.
3. A $100M Money Model eliminates cash as the bottleneck for growth.
