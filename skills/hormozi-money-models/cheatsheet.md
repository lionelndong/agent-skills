# Cheatsheet — $100M Money Models

## The one definition
**A Money Model is a sequence of offers.** Goal: make enough from one customer to **get and service at least two more in <30 days** → cash stops being the bottleneck.

## The Four Types of Offers → The Three Stages
| Offer type | Job | Stage |
|---|---|---|
| **Attraction** | Turn strangers into customers | **I — Get Cash** |
| **Upsell** | Get people to spend more | **II — Get More Cash** |
| **Downsell** | Turn "no" into "yes" | **II — Get More Cash** |
| **Continuity** | Keep people buying | **III — Get The Most Cash** |

## Build order (4 steps)
Attraction → Upsell → Downsell → Continuity. **Perfect one offer at a time** (reliable → automatic → next stage). Each stage pays for the next. Measure in quarters.

## Compounding
2x value × 2x customers × 2x speed = **8x** growth. 3x each = **27x**.

## Attraction Offers (free/discount; "free" = 100% discount)
| Play | One-liner |
|---|---|
| **Win Your Money Back** | Do X in Y time by Z rules → get it free; criteria must advertise the business |
| **Giveaway** | Grand Prize (price anchor) → everyone else gets the discounted "promo offer" |
| **Decoy Offer** | Stripped decoy beside a premium; "free stuff or lasting results?" |
| **Buy X Get Y Free** | Reframe a discount as free; give more free than paid; raise price first |
| **Pay Less Now / Pay More Later** | Pay later (delayed + conditional guarantee) or pay now (20–50% off + bonuses) |
*Discount offers = fewer leads, higher show-up rates than free. Discount 4 ways: % off · $ off · free portion · package price.*

## Upsell Offers (more / better / new — maximize 30-day profit)
| Play | Move |
|---|---|
| **Classic** | "You can't have X without Y" — solve the next problem now; offer the more profitable one first |
| **Menu** | Unsell → Prescribe → A/B → Card On File |
| **Anchor** | Premium (5–10x) first → "the gasp" → main offer looks cheap |
| **Rollover** | Credit prior purchase toward a pricier offer (price it ≥4x the credit) |
*Tells: say "no" to say "yes" · faster access = more value · BAMFAM · name bundles · charge for guarantees/warranties.*

## Downsell Offers (offer made after a "no")
- **Payment Plan** (how they pay — same product, spread cost)
- **Trial With Penalty** (how they pay — lower up-front commitment)
- **Feature Downsell** (what they get — lower quantity/quality / peel features)
*Rules: they said no to THIS offer, not all · downsells are trades · personalize don't pressure · never discount "just because" (customers talk) · alternate plays in one sale.*

## Continuity Offers (sell once, get paid repeatedly — do LAST)
- **Continuity Bonus** (bonus worth > first payment) · **Continuity Discount** · **Waived Fee** · + urgency.
- Math: $1,000 once → 10 buyers = $10k. Same value at $50/mo → 40 buyers × 20 mo = $2k now + **$40k later** + 4x customers to upsell.

## Important notes
- **Raise price in stages** — start cheap for yeses + feedback, raise until you make *less* money.
- **Simple Scales, Fancy Fails** — "100 ways to offer your product," not 100 products.
- **Affiliate products fill Money Model gaps** (no delivery headache).
- **Turn Attraction into Continuity** via automatic renewal.
- **Mix and match** — there are no rules.

## Where it fits
$100M **Offers** = *what to sell* · $100M **Leads** = *how to find buyers* · $100M **Money Models** = *how to get them to buy*.
